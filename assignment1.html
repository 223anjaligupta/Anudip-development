Durga Puja is a major festival celebrating the victory of Goddess Durga over Mahishasura. The city of Kolkata is known for beautiful pandals and lights.

3. Uttar Pradesh - Deepawali

Deepawali is a 4-day-long Diwali is celebrated in India to symbolize the victory of light over darkness, good over evil, and knowledge over ignorance, marking significant events like Lord Rama's return to Ayodhya and the worship of Goddess Lakshmi for prosperity, with festivities including lighting lamps and sharing sweets.
